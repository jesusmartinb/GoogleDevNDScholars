Cheetah

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Speed: 110 - 120 km/h very short periods
  • Body mass: 21 - 72 Kg
  • Height: 66 - 94 cm
  • Length: 1.1 - 1.5 m

The cheetah owes its enormous speed to its huge strides, which allow it to cover a lot of ground at each step. It has very long legs, and the length of its stride increases thanks to a flexible column. When it runs at high speed, it is bent so that the hind legs advance; then it stretches in the opposite direction to get that huge stride.
